Dog Bath & Grooming or Touch up Services

We provide all services from the comfort of our state-of-art mobile grooming salon, which we will gladly let you tour our work space when we arrive. Please note that the listed prices below are for pets that have been groomed on a regular basis.

Full Bath

starting at $45

 

Our full-service bath includes:

  • thorough cleaning with the Super Suds Bathing system and specialty shampoo and conditioner
  • blow out and brush
  • trim Paw Pads
  • clean up Sani area if needed
  • nail grinding
  • clean eyes and ears (pull ear hair if needed)
  • brush teeth with breathe freshener
  • bow, tie or bandana 
  • finishing spray
  • spritz of cologne (with approval)
  • tasty treat (with approval)

Full Bath & Grooming

starting at $75

Our full grooming service includes all Full Bath services as well as:

  • breed specific cut
  • spray conditioner
  • de-matting (additional charge may apply)
